    Teaching at Middle East Technical University

    (as a Lecturer)

    PHIL 442: Contemporary Philosophy II

    Topics Included: Foundations of Logical Positivism, Logical Positivism, the Carnap-Quine debate, Quine on Ontology and Modality, Modal Realism.

    PHIL 405: Philosophy of Language

    Topics Included: Frege, Russell, Kripke, Quine, Grice, Austin, Empiricist Theories of Meaning, Psychological Theories of Meaning, Speech Acts.

    PHIL 419: Problems in Philosophy II

    Topics Included: The Key Notions and Problems in Modality, Possible Worlds, Lewisian Modal Realism, Plantingan Actualist Realism, Classical Conventionalism, The Necessary A Posteriori, Truth-By-Convention, Neo-Conventionalism.

    Teaching at King's College London

    (as a Teaching Assistant and First Exam Marker)

    5AANB016: Intermediate Logic

    Topics Included: Induction, Recursion, Semantics of and Natural Deduction for First-Order Logic, The Cut Elimination Theorem, Soundness, Completeness, Compactness, Löwenheim-Skolem Theorem.

    4AANA003: Elementary Logic

    Topics Included: Syntax, Semantics and Gentzen-Style Natural Deduction for Propositional Logic and First-Order Logic, Introduction to Soundness and Completeness.

    5AANB011: Philosophy of Language

    Topics Included: Frege, Russell, Grice, Kripke, Lying and Misleading, Speech Acts, Silencing, Slurs.

    4AANA004: Metaphysics I

    Topics Included: Personal Identity, Change, Theories of Time, Causation, Free Will, Determinism, Metametaphysics.
