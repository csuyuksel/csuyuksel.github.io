    • I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Middle East Technical University, working on the TÜBİTAK-funded project “Hyperintensionality in Plato’s Metaphysics of Forms.” I received my PhD in Philosophy from King’s College London in October 2022, after completing an MA in Philosophy at King’s and a BA in Philosophy at Panthéon-Sorbonne University.


    My research is at the intersection of metaphysics, philosophy of language, and logic, with a particular focus on modality. I am especially interested in the nature of modal truth—what makes a modal claim true, and what kinds of truthmakers can account for its truth.
